EuroSciPy: overview

"cross-disciplinary gathering focused on the use and development of Python in scientific research"

  • European flavour of SciPy conference in US
  • First conference in 2008, Leipzig
    • Brussels, Paris, Cambridge, Erlangen
  • 2 days of tutorials (beginner/advance tracks)
    • numpy, matplotlib, pandas, scikit-learn...
  • 2 days of talks
  • Poster session with lightning talks
  • 1 day of sprints
